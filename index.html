In this project, we investigate essential concepts related to training Machine Learning algorithms, optimizing parameters, and defining critical boundaries between overfitting and underfitting in various tasks, including classification, regression, and clustering. These parameters play a crucial role in controlling overfitting and underfitting phenomena.
